Building a Cross-Platform Connection Tool
–

Connecting with college coaches often brings nerves and confusion for high school athletes, whether high performance or average players. Navigating this rule-heavy and daunting process often prevents many student-athletes from playing sports in college. SportsHi set out to create a simple and direct tool for connecting student-athletes with college coaches which addresses the systemic problems from both sides.

Student Connection Form

Due to NCAA rules, coaches are limited in their outreach to student-athletes; conversely, students can freely reach out to coaches and recruiters. Students often do not know in what manner to approach coaches or what to provide. We've learned coaches only need three pieces of information to begin a recruitment conversation with a student: sport, school year, and scores. We've built a simpler solution asking only for the pertinent information.

Key takeaways:

We've seen success with our tool for students and coaches. Our users have gone onto get full-ride scholarships at top universities.

Using our mobile platform appeals to high schoolers and brings ease to the recruitment process. Engaging coaches (often less tech-savvy) over email brings athlete information to their most familiar platform, email.

Smaller universities, including D2 and D3 schools, often have trouble finding athletes and have begun reaching out to SportsHi. This is a major revenue opportunity for SportsHi.
