SportsHi From Ideation to Launch
–

We set out the create the home of high school sports. After a simple MVP and many iterations thereafter, our team has created a dynamic product serving the interests of athletes, coaches, and fans. While we started as a team management app, SportsHi's primary focus became communication among players and teams, simple connection to college coaches, and high school sports content. Our mission is to become the LinkedIn of high school sports with a Gen Z focus.


History

The founder of SportsHi approached me at the end of 2016 to help create a quick brand identity (which would serve as a placeholder for what would become SportsHi) to pitch a concept he had for a high school sports app.

Problem

Gen Z live their life online but not with high school sports. When they do choose engage with sports online they are using many platforms.

Solution

Create a single platform for high schoolers to connect with their team and other athletes, post sports content, and easily connect with college coaches.

From concept to pitch

Before I first began prototyping SportsHi, I created a pitch deck to communicate the concept to advisors and potential investors in order to learn and grow the idea. Over the years, there has been hundreds of iterations of the deck. They have sucessfully helped secure funding from angels and funds, including 37 Angels which I played an integral role in pitching and diligence. See our most recent pitch deck here.

Research & Usability Tests


From the beginning, we met and talked with student athletes to understand what they're missing and need regarding a high school sports app. We have repeatedly met with boys from Fordham Prep and girls from Columbia Secondary School to focus group and usability test prototypes, the MVP, and subsequent iterations.
