SportsHi From Ideation to Launch
–

We set out the create the home of high school sports. After a simple MVP and many iterations thereafter, our team has created a dynamic product serving the interests of athletes, coaches, and fans. While we started as a team management app, SportsHi's primary focuses became communication among players and teams, college connections, and high school sports content. Our mission is to become the LinkedIn of high school sports with a Gen Z focus.
